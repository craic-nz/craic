# Disposal Review Form

> A form to review and approve a proposed disposal of records before any destruction or transfer takes place.

- **Purpose:** To provide a documented check that a proposed disposal is authorised, that no legal hold or active request applies, and that the action has been reviewed and approved by the appropriate roles before it is carried out. The completed form is the evidence that disposal was properly authorised.
- **Owner / completed by:** [RECORDS MANAGER] (reviewed and approved as set out below)
- **Related legislation / standards:**
  - Public Records Act 2005: disposal of public records requires the authority of the Chief Archivist. See Archives New Zealand disposal guidance: https://www.archives.govt.nz/manage-information
  - Privacy Act 2020 (secure disposal of personal information): https://www.privacy.org.nz/privacy-act-2020/

## How to use this template

- Complete one form per disposal action or per coherent batch of records being disposed of together.
- Confirm the disposal authority reference before proposing the action; records with no current authority must not be disposed of.
- Carry out the legal hold and active request check, and record the result. If a hold applies, the disposal must not proceed.
- Obtain reviewer and approver sign-off before any destruction or transfer, and keep the completed form as a permanent record of the action.

## Notes

- Disposal must not proceed where a legal hold or active request applies, even if a disposal authority otherwise covers the records.
- Personal information must be disposed of securely so that it cannot be reconstructed or recovered.
- Retain the completed form, together with any certificate of destruction or transfer acknowledgement, as evidence of authorised disposal.
- This is a general template to be adapted to your agency's policies and disposal authorities; it is not legal advice.
