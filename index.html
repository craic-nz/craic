what this is

A free resource, not a sales pitch

craic.nz is an independent public-good project. There is nothing to buy and nothing to sign up for. The aim is simply to make good information-management practice a little easier to find and reuse.

Everything is written to be generic. Where it draws on official frameworks, those are attributed and linked. Contributions and corrections are welcome by email.

Get in touch

Plain terms. This site is a general resource for information professionals. It is not legal advice and it is not endorsed by any government agency.

Material that references New Zealand Government frameworks (such as the NZISM, the Public Records Act 2005 and the Privacy Act 2020) is Crown copyright, attributed to its source. Always check the current official version.
